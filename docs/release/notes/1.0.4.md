# HoldType 1.0.4

This release makes longer dictation sessions safer and gives you control over
when HoldType finishes a recording automatically.

## Changes

- Choose a maximum recording length from 1 to 15 minutes in Behavior settings.
  The default remains five minutes, and changes apply to the next recording.
- Reaching the selected limit now finishes the recording normally and continues
  transcription instead of rejecting the completed audio.
- The floating recording indicator and menu bar now show a countdown during the
  final minute, with clearer warnings as the limit approaches.
- Completed recordings are protected in History before transcription begins, so
  interrupted or failed attempts can be played, retried, or deleted without
  recording them again.

## Fixed

- Made recording finalization reliable when the recorder, keyboard shortcut,
  and duration limit finish at nearly the same time.
- Recovered cleanly when macOS misses the release of the Right Command shortcut,
  preventing recording from remaining active unexpectedly.
- Preserved non-empty completed audio when recording ends unexpectedly and kept
  it available in History while transcription continues.
